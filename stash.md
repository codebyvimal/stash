# Tally

**Motto / Core Idea:** 
"Turn your daily tasks into a personal economy." 
Tally is a personal self-gamification and productivity rewards app.

**Why it exists:**
Tally acts as a personal economy for motivation—part habit tracker, part reward system. It helps build discipline by ensuring you only get to enjoy rewards you've actually earned. Your balance serves as the live score of your discipline.

**How it works (The Core Loop):**
1. **Plan:** You plan tasks ahead of time and assign a point value to each task.
2. **Complete:** You mark tasks complete when done. This initiates a strict 6-hour credit countdown.
3. **Earn:** Points are credited to your balance only after the 6-hour window elapses. This rule acts as a delayed gratification mechanism.
4. **Spend:** You spend your credited points to claim real-life rewards you've defined (e.g., watch a movie, play games, buy something).
